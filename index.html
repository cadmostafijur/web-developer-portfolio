Jr. Vinicius

Not to be confused with Carlos Vinícius. In this Portuguese name, the first or maternal family name is Paixão and the second or paternal family name is de Oliveira. "Júnior" is a generational suffix used for someone whose name is the same as their father, like "Jr." in English.
